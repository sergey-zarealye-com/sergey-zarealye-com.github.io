Frequently Asked Questions

Here is how to use our Proper Property Listing bot

Join @ProperListingBot

Getting advise on your photo

  • Just upload your photo to @ProperListingBot and our AI will do the rest
  • A large AI model will analyze your photo for general photography mistakes and specific issues commonly associated with real estate promotional imagery
  • There are many rules, such as avoiding pets in property photos and balancing visual clutter with empty space in a scene. Our AI is trained on all of these rules
  • Your photo analysis results and advice will be displayed with additional buttons:
  • Use the "Don’t Agree" button to re-evaluate your photo. AI is known for what is often referred to as the "hallucination" effect, which can occasionally lead to inaccurate results. Re-evaluating your photo will apply slightly different AI parameters, which may result in a more accurate judgment.
  • Use the "Remove Photo" button to delete the photo and all its associated descriptions. Please note that, in accordance with Telegram's policies, photos in the bot can only be deleted within one to two days of their upload time.

Getting an optimal listing photos

  1. Our AI evaluates the rating for each photo you upload and uses a sophisticated optimization model to select the best photos for your listing
  2. An optimal listing should include the best photos for each major category of your property, such as rooms, bathrooms, exterior views, etc.
  3. This will work best if you upload a few dozen photos. Feel free to take multiple photos of the same room from slightly different angles and let the AI choose the best ones.
  4. To create an optimal listing, please press the 'Get Listing' button or type /listing command
  5. By default, all photos you upload on the current day will be considered for an optimal listing
  6. It is possible that you have uploaded irrelevant photos today. To exclude them from listing optimization, you can delete individual photos using the 'Erase Photo' button.
  7. You can also remove all photos uploaded today by simply typing the /clear command.
  8. Your listing results will be displayed with three additional buttons: 'Yesterday', 'This Week', and 'Last Week'. Use these buttons to obtain an optimized listing for photos uploaded during the corresponding time periods.

Making payments and refunds

  1. First of all, @ProperListingBot is free to try!
  2. You will receive a generous welcome bonus when you start using the bot. The bonus amount will allow you to process several dozen photos free of charge.
  3. If you decide to continue using the bot, please type the /donate command. This will open the standard Telegram interface to transfer your Telegram Stars to the bot's account.
  4. You may donate any amount starting from 1 Star. Use the command /donate XX, where XX is the amount in Stars.
  5. Your donations are non-refundable. However, in the event of a technical error, you will be provided with a transaction code. In such cases, please use the /refund ID command, where ID is your transaction ID. Your Stars payment will then be returned to your account.

Chatting with the @ProperListingBot

  1. When you start using the bot, you can select a language that is comfortable for you by pressing the corresponding country flag.
  2. You can change the language setting at any time by typing the /start command again.
  3. You are welcome to chat with the bot: simply send a text message. The AI will respond to you, but please do not take its responses too seriously.
  4. English is the default working language for our AI. However, you can translate your photo analysis results into your selected language. You will find a 'Translate' button below the photo advice.
