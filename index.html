Selecting the best photos for listing

It is not efficient to include dozens of pictures in a property listing. Too many images may distract potential customers. Once you upload enough photos of your property to @ProperListingBot, it will intelligently select the best image pack for optimal property promotion.

Photography retouching

While the more saturated photo with warm yellow tones may seem more appealing to you, in real estate photography, it is more appropriate to use a neutral color palette. A common mistake in unprofessional property listings is presenting different rooms with varying color schemes. @ProperListingBot automatically performs image color balancing to meet industry expectations.
